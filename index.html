Our Story

Where AI Meets Clinical Research Excellence

Intelligent Solutions Discovery (ISD) was founded with a single conviction — that the future of clinical research in Saudi Arabia must be powered by intelligence, precision, and purpose.

Our focus lies in providing end-to-end solutions for clinical research: data analysis, trial optimization, clinical trials training, and personalized drug development. We bridge the gap between global best practices and the unique needs of the Saudi healthcare ecosystem.
